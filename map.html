Wallachtia & Klanansk

Wallcarus

The capital of the Wallachtian Empire and its largest city.

Froenberg

The industrious "City of Smokestacks" at the northern edge of Wallachtia.

Acharos

"The City of Milk and Honey" sits on the Vitrean Lake and is a popular destination for nobles and wealthy merchants.

 🏠 Stillwater

Stillwater

A small fishing hamlet nestled where the freshwater runoff of the Vitrean Lake runs into the sea.

Voldt

A small city known primarily for breeding the finest warhorses in Wallachtia.

Wronsk

A small city that has served as the staging grounds for most of the Wallachtian military during the war.

Holton

A Wallachtian city known for producing fish and hosting hunting parties to the Royal Woods to the east.

Stoneriver

A town once known for hosting travellers across the subcontinent, it has borne the brunt of the conflict and is a shadow of its former self.

Tar-Mok

A small Klanash city on the northern coast of the Vitrean Lake, the city links much trade between Klanansk and Wallachtia.
