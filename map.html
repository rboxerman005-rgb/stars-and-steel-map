Wallachtia & Klanansk

Wallcarus

The capital of the Wallachtian Empire and its largest city.

Froenberg

The industrious "City of Smokestacks" at the northern edge of Wallachtia.

Acharos

"The City of Milk and Honey" sits on the Vitrean Lake and is a popular destination for nobles and wealthy merchants.

 ▪️ Stillwater

Stillwater

A small fishing hamlet nestled where the freshwater runoff of the Vitrean Lake runs into the sea.

Voldt

A small city known primarily for breeding the finest warhorses in Wallachtia.

Wronsk

A small city that has served as the staging grounds for most of the Wallachtian military during the war.

Holton

A Wallachtian city known for producing fish and hosting hunting parties to the Royal Woods to the east.

Stoneriver

A town once known for hosting travellers across the subcontinent, it has borne the brunt of the conflict and is a shadow of its former self.

Tar-Mok

A small Klanash city on the northern coast of the Vitrean Lake, the city links much trade between Klanansk and Wallachtia.

Dörmek

"The City of Hammers," the Dwarven stronghold of Klanansk, sits on an active volcano and produces some of the finest smithed wares in the known world.

Sveln

A Klanash settlement known for producing most of the mithril from the country.

Umiryushi

This city in the mist is home to the fearsome dragonborn navy of Klanansk.

Tar-Guluk

The Wandering City of Tar-Guluk is the capital of Klanansk, and a sacred place for all Klanash citizens.

 🌲

The Emerald Sea

A huge, dense forest known as The Emerald Sea. Most residents of the subcontinent are leery of the forest, as rumors claim that it is a cursed place home to malevolent fey spirits.

 🌾

Thundering Plains

A vast grassland in the heart of Klanansk, named for the thunderous herds of massive tortoises that migrate across it.

 🌊

Vitrean Lake

The largest body of water on the subcontinent, a popular retreat for wealthy Wallachtians — though most avoid its eastern shores.

 🌊

Mithril Lake

A deep lake in Klanansk named for the rich mithril deposits on its lakebed, home to the city of Sveln.

 🌊

Dragon's Eye

A massive whirlpool in the Dragon's Claw that perpetually churns the waters near Umiryushi, recently growing more turbulent than usual.

 🌾

Golden Expanse

A vast stretch of extraordinarily fertile farmland that forms the agricultural backbone of the Wallachtian Empire.

 🌲

Royal Woods

A lush forest reserve in Wallachtia reserved for noble hunts — common folk who enter are punishable by death.

 🌊

Bay of Acarius

A calm, sheltered bay where the Sigin River meets the sea, named after Acarius, the founder of Wallachtia.

 🌊

Shattered Isles

A treacherous chain of rocky islands controlled by Klanansk, frustrating Wallachtian attempts to navigate the western seaboard.

 ⛰️

Grey Hills

Barren, ash-covered hills stretching to the western sea, said to be scarred by an ancient battle of devastating power.

 ⛰️

Bundush Mountains

A towering mountain range forming the northwestern corner of the subcontinent, home to the dwarven city of Dormek.

 ⛰️

Giant's Finger

The highest and snowiest peaks of the Bundush Mountains, where legend says giants once roamed in ancient times.

 ⛰️

Dragon's Claw

A chain of rocky islands leading to the dragonborn city of Umiryushi, navigable only by a single treacherous clifftop road.

 🌊

The Fork

The point where the Sigin River splits to form the Lasiva River, where the city of Stoneriver sits on its western banks.
